Welcome

Welcome to the Pridesports Privacy Policy for https://pridesports.in/ (the “Website”). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use and disclose information from our users, including with respect to the Website and its related content, applications, services, tools and features (collectively with the Website, the “Services”).

By using, accessing, or downloading anything on the Services, you agree to our Terms of Service and this Privacy Policy.

1. Scope of Services

connect Clients and their children with the right sports academy in their vicinity, ensuring they have a great experience through our experience management platform.

  1. 1.1 Listing Services
  • Display academy offerings, facilities, and programs.
  • Connect directly with Clients seeking academies.
  • Engage with potential clients via platform features.
  1. 1.2 Experience Management Services
  • Complaint Management: Clients can report issues and communicate with academies.
  • Reports: child’s progress via periodic reports.
  • Reviews & Ratings: Clients can leave authentic feedback.
  • Events: View and register for academy events and competitions.

2. Types of Information We Collect

We may collect personal and non-personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Client details (name, email, phone, child’s age, preferences).
  • Academy details (name, programs, contact info, reports).
  • Usage data (device, IP, browser, clickstream, cookies).
  • Purchase/content history, interactions, and analytics data.

3. How We Collect Your Information

We collect information directly from Clients and Academies, automatically through usage, communications, and sometimes from third-party integrations such as social media.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Services.
  • Process transactions and manage accounts.
  • Personalize recommendations and experiences.
  • Send confirmations, invoices, updates, and alerts.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized use, or illegal activity.

5. Data Transfer

We may share your information with affiliates, vendors, and service providers strictly for service delivery, always ensuring compliance with applicable data protection laws.
