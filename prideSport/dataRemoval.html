Data Removal Request

If you wish to have your personal data removed from our system, please follow the instructions below.

1. Instructions for Data Deletion

  • Send an email to contact@pridesports.in with the subject line: "Data Removal Request".
  • In the body of the email, please include the mobile number registered with your Pride Sports account.
  • You may also include a reason for your request, though it is optional.

2. Data Privacy Information

At Pride Sports, we take your privacy seriously. All personal data is stored in an encrypted manner to ensure its security.

We do not share your data with any third parties under any circumstances. Your data is used solely to enhance your experience with our services.

3. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your data or the removal process, please contact us at:

Email: contact@pridesports.in
